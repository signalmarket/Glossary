Japanese reading of "New York Mercantile Exchange", another name for "New York Mercantile Exchange". It is also called "NYMEX" for short. The New York Mercantile Exchange is one of the major exchanges of the CME Group in New York, U.S.A. It lists futures on crude oil, natural gas, precious metals, etc. The WTI crude oil futures contract is famous as a representative index of crude oil prices. COMEX became a division of NYMEX.
