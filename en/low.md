One of the risk management indicators (risk factors) for options trading, this indicator expresses how much the option price (premium) changes with changes in interest rates. It is expressed as the change in the option price in response to a 1% change in the short-term interest rate. Formula: Roe = Change in option price รท Change in short-term interest rate
