title: conversion factor
tags: 
---

In JGB futures trading, the conversion ratio between the standard issue to be traded and the delivery-eligible issue used for actual delivery. It is the Japanese reading of the English term "Conversion Factor. It is used to adjust for differences in coupon rates and remaining maturities of delivery-eligible issues.
