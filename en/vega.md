One of the risk management indicators (risk factors) for options trading, it is an indicator of how much the option price (premium) changes when the implied volatility (expected volatility) of the underlying asset moves. The value of Vega tends to move in a manner similar to gamma, with the value increasing near at-the-money and approaching zero as it moves in-the-money or out-of-the-money.
